About Us

Nangchen Nyernga is a non-profit organization started by Dukdak Nyima, the President and Tenzin the Vice President in 1999. It was one of the first non-profit organization in our Tibetan Community in New York and New Jersey at the time.The goal of our organization is same as that of all other NGOs, that is, work towards public and for the social Wellfare of our Community. If you have any questions regarding our social service, please contact our current President, Tashi Nyima and Vice President Nyangtsa Penpa.
